About TWJ

A quiet, focused classroom — wherever you are

"Whoever learns the Qur'an and teaches it — that is the best among you."

TWJ Quran Academy was built around one idea: correct recitation and true memorization need a real teacher, real attention, and real consistency — not a crowded classroom.

Every student is taught one-on-one, live online, whether they're just starting Nazra or working through the Hifz journey, Juz by Juz.

Founded and led by Fatima Ali.

Courses

Two paths, one foundation

Course 01

Nazra-e-Qur'an

Learn to read the Qur'an correctly from the very first letter, with proper Tajweed rules, pronunciation (makharij), and fluency — at a pace matched to the student's age and level.

Format · Online, 1:1For · Kids & adults
Course 02

Hifz-e-Qur'an

Structured memorization of the Qur'an, Juz by Juz, with daily Sabaq (new lesson), Sabqi (recent revision), and Manzil (long-term revision) to make memorization permanent.

Format · Online, 1:1For · Kids & adults
The Hifz Path

30 Juz. One block at a time.

Every Hifz student's journey moves through the same 30 Juz. Each one lit below represents a stage of the path our students walk — Sabaq, Sabqi, and Manzil, repeated until it stays.
